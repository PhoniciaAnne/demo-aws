Roadmap

Planned Enhancements

Q1 2025
Multi-Region Active-Passive ✅
DynamoDB Global Tables with us-east-1 as primary writer and us-west-2 as warm standby. Replication lag under 200ms.
Q2 2025
Automated Remediation Pipeline ✅
AWS Health → EventBridge → Lambda → Route53 health-check inversion. Fully automated failover under 5 min RTO with zero manual intervention.
Q3 2025
SLO Observability Integration 🔄
Nobl9 error-budget alerting integrated with PagerDuty. Burn-rate alerting at 2× and 14× thresholds across 30-day rolling windows.
Q4 2025
Active-Active Architecture
Migrate to true active-active with DynamoDB Global Tables write forwarding enabled. Reduce RTO to near-zero with traffic split via Route53 weighted routing.
Q1 2026
Chaos Engineering Suite
AWS FIS integration for continuous resilience validation. Quarterly GameDays with scheduled outage experiments across all 9 service tiers.
